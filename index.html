Ascension Blade

A craftable weapon with a custom scythe texture.

Killing a player with it steals one of their rebirth levels, including the ability tied to it. The stolen ability is stored as an item in your inventory.

When you activate it, your currently equipped ability is removed and replaced.

To acquire someone's rebirth and their tied ability, you must kill them using the Ascension Blade.

×

Rebirths

How to Rebirth

Requires 1 Rebirth Point, earned by:

  • Reaching the required kill streak without dying
  • Purchasing from the server store
  • Crafting a rebirth point using its custom recipe

Upon completing a rebirth, players are presented with a selection of three abilities, from which they may permanently choose one.

Rebirth Broadcast System

  • Rebirth 1 (first time): [username] has reached his first rebirth
  • Rebirth 6: [username] has reached his last rebirth

The 18 Abilities

Ascension I — Low Power

  • Iron Skin: Grants Resistance I and reduces incoming damage for 8s. Cooldown: 40s.
  • Dash: Launches you forward with cloud particle trail. Cooldown: 15s.
  • Smokescreen: Blinds the target and surrounds them in smoke for 3s. Cooldown: 45s.

Ascension II — Low Power

  • Blood Rush: On hit/being hit — Speed II for 5s. Cooldown: 45s.
  • Fortify: On being hit — reduces 25% incoming damage for 8s. Cooldown: 50s.
  • Splinter: Next attacks deal +20% bonus damage for 10s. Cooldown: 50s.

Ascension III — Medium Power

  • Exposed: Target takes +50% bonus damage for 4s. Cooldown: 60s.
  • Phantom Step: Invisibility and portal particles for 4s. Cooldown: 65s.
  • Shockwave: Knocks back all enemies in 5 blocks and deals 2 hearts. Cooldown: 55s.

Ascension IV — Medium Power

  • Overclock: Speed II, Haste I, full projectile immunity for 6s. Cooldown: 70s.
  • Soul Drain: Strips all active potion effects from target. Cooldown: 75s.
  • Warden's Grasp: Roots target in place with Slowness X for 3s. Cooldown: 80s.

Ascension V — High Power

  • Wraith Form: Invisibility, cleanses debuffs, cancels all incoming damage for 5s. Blocks Reaper's Call and Warden's Grasp. Cooldown: 450s.
  • Execution: Hit an enemy below 25% HP within 10s for 2x damage. Cooldown: 85s.
  • Marked: Target takes +40% damage from all sources for 5s. Cooldown: 95s.

Ascension VI — High Power

  • Soul Lock: Silences target — strips effects and prevents item use for 6s. Cooldown: 115s.
  • Warlord's Aura: Resistance I and Regen I for you and all allies in 5 blocks for 8s. Cooldown: 110s.
  • Reaper's Call: Encases target in 4 black rings — frozen, blinded, nauseous, hotbar locked for 4s. Hits deal True Damage ignoring armor. Blocked by Wraith Form. Cooldown: 300s.

Streak Rules

  • Killing the same player has a 30-minute cooldown before it counts again.
  • Dying resets your streak to 0.
  • Streak does not reset on logout.
  • Active streaks are publicly visible to all players.

Display System

  • Under 5 kills — nothing shown
  • 5–14 kills — [X 🔥] in orange
  • 15–24 kills — [X 🔥] in purple
  • 25–30 kills — [X 🔥] in blood red, slightly larger

Plugin Information

The main idea of this plugin is for players that love PvP, wars and battles — so they can express skill and strength. But it's also made for players that love grinding, want to avoid PvP, and just want to live in peace while feeling and being powerful. Ascension is all about ascending from being nothing, to being everything.

Concept

Ascension is a PvP & grinding-based plugin where players must either reach a specific kill streak to earn Rebirth Points — unlocking various abilities — or grind for them. You can also earn Rebirth Points by crafting them using the custom recipe found in our Discord.

When rebirthed, you're presented with a menu and 3 abilities to choose 1 from. With each rebirth, the abilities get stronger. There are 18 abilities and 6 rebirth levels, with more planned for the future.

Commands & Utilities

⚔️ Combat & Mechanics

CommandWhat it does
/streakOpens a GUI showing your current kill streak and progress toward the next Rebirth Point.
/streak-toggleToggles your streak tag visibility above your head.
/rebirthOpens the Rebirth Panel to spend Rebirth Points and select abilities.
/ability-set <player> <ability>(Admin) Forcefully sets a player's active ability.
