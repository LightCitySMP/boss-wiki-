Boss I — Tier 1
Solar Arbiter
The Scorched Aggressor
Mechanics
Environment Eater
Consumes nearby grass and dirt blocks to fuel HP regeneration.
Summon Swarm
Spawns 3–5 Blaze-like Solar Flares that swarm and harass players.
Sacrificial Pact
Marks a player with Solar Burn — they glow gold for 60s. If they die while marked, the boss heals 5% HP.
Visuals
Weapon Glow
Sword emits a high-intensity orange light at all times.
Impact Craters
Heavy slams leave scorch mark particles burned into the ground.
Heartbeat Pulse
Rhythmic orange particle bursts radiate from the boss's center.
Atmosphere
Ash Rain
Grey particles drift down from the sky throughout the entire fight.
Heavy Breathing
Intense fire and breath sounds play during boss idle and cooldowns.
Thunder Without Rain
Distant rumbling begins once the boss drops below 50% HP.

Boss II — Tier 2
Lunar Arbiter
The Void Stalker
Mechanics
Mirror Image
Spawns 3 translucent decoy clones every 30 seconds to confuse players.
Shadow Step
Instantly teleports behind whichever player is dealing the most DPS.
Projectile Reflection
Periodically raises a void shield that reflects incoming arrows back at players.
Visuals
Speed Lines
Visual streaks trail behind the boss during every rapid teleport.
Reality Warp
Nearby blocks hum and vibrate with purple particles, distorting the environment.
Energy Beams
Connects to targeted players during specific attacks to drain their vision.
Atmosphere
Shadow Shapes
Dark silhouettes flicker in the distance around the arena perimeter.
Whispers in Chat
Sends cryptic, private messages one-on-one to nearby players mid-fight.
Distant Screams
Eerie audio cues play on every phase transition.

Boss III — Tier 3
Nebular Arbiter
The Cosmic Architect
Mechanics
Gravity Well
Pulls all players within 12 blocks toward the boss's center.
Time Dilation
A 5-block bubble slows player movement speed and all projectile velocity.
True Sight
Boss turns invisible periodically; players must track its faint star trail to find it.
Visuals
Constellation Trails
Leaves particles forming star patterns as it moves around the arena.
Floating Shards
Orbiting crystals spin increasingly fast the lower its HP drops.
Starlight Rain
Gentle white particles fall from the sky throughout the entire fight.
Atmosphere
Ambient Hum
A low-frequency cosmic vibration plays continuously for all nearby players.
Gravity Sound
Heavy thud sounds play every time a gravity ability activates.
Purple Rain
All rain is replaced by purple end-rod particles for the entire fight.
