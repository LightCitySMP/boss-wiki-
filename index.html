Ascension Blade

A craftable weapon with a custom scythe texture.

Killing a player with it steals one of their rebirth levels, including the ability tied to it. The stolen ability is stored as an item in your inventory.

When you activate it, your currently equipped ability is removed and replaced.

The way to acquire someone's rebirth and the tied ability using the blade, you must kill the person used the ascension blade.

Rebirths

How to Rebirth

Requires 1 Rebirth Point, earned by:

  • Reaching the required kill streak without dying
  • Purchasing from the server store
  • You can also craft a rebirth point using its custom recipe

Upon completing a rebirth, players are presented with a selection of three abilities, from which they may permanently choose one.

Rebirth Broadcast System

  • When a player reaches Rebirth 1 for the first time, broadcast to the entire server: [username] has reached his first rebirth
  • When a player reaches Rebirth 6, broadcast: [username] has reached his last rebirth

The 18 Abilities

Ascension I — Low Power

  • Iron Skin: Grants Resistance I and reduces incoming damage for 8 seconds. Cooldown: 40s.
  • Dash: Launches the player forward in the direction they are looking, leaving a trail of cloud particles. Instant. Cooldown: 15s.
  • Smokescreen: Blinds the target player (or yourself if no target is found) and surrounds them in thick campfire smoke for 3 seconds. Cooldown: 45s.

Ascension II — Low Power

  • Blood Rush: Passive/reactive. When you hit someone or get hit, instantly gain Speed II for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 45s.
  • Fortify: Passive/reactive, when you get hit, it reduces 25% of incoming damage for 8 seconds, cooldown: 50 seconds.
  • Splinter: Empowers your next attacks to deal +20% bonus damage for 10 seconds, emitting critical hit particles. Cooldown: 50s.

Ascension III — Medium Power

  • Exposed: Curses a target, causing them to take +50% bonus damage from all sources for 4 seconds. Cooldown: 60s.
  • Phantom Step: Grants Invisibility and emits portal particles for 4 seconds. Cooldown: 65s.
  • Shockwave: Violently knocks back all enemies within a 5-block radius and deals 4.0 damage (2 hearts), leaving a purple dust ring. Instant. Cooldown: 55s.

Ascension IV — Medium Power

  • Overclock: Grants Speed II, Haste I, and complete immunity to all projectiles for 6 seconds. Cooldown: 70s.
  • Soul Drain: Instantly strips all active potion effects from the target. A yellow ring and soul particles surround them. Cooldown: 75s.
  • Warden's Grasp: Roots the target in place completely and applies Slowness X for 3 seconds. Can be blocked by Wraith Form. Cooldown: 80s.

Ascension V — High Power

  • Wraith Form: Grants Invisibility, cleanses Blindness/Nausea/Slowness, cancels all incoming damage, and blocks/removes Reaper's Call and Warden's Grasp for 5 seconds. Summons an Allay on your shoulder. Cooldown: 450s.
  • Execution: If you hit an enemy below 25% HP within a 10-second window, deal 2x damage accompanied by lightning effects. Cooldown: 85s.
  • Marked: Targets an enemy, causing them to take +40% bonus damage from all sources for 5 seconds. Cooldown: 95s.

Ascension VI — High Power

  • Soul Lock: Silences the target. Strips all their potion effects and prevents them from consuming any food or items for 6 seconds. Cooldown: 115s.
  • Warlord's Aura: Grants Resistance I and Regeneration I to yourself and all allies within 5 blocks for 8 seconds. Indicated by heart particles. Cooldown: 110s.
  • Reaper's Call: Encases the target in 4 spinning black rings. Target is frozen, blinded, and nauseous. Their hotbar is locked with red glass panes preventing item use. Hitting the target during this phase deals True Damage ignoring all armor. Blocked by Wraith Form. Lasts 4 seconds. Cooldown: 300s.

Streak Rules

  • Killing the same player requires a 30 minute cooldown before it counts again.
  • Dying resets your streak to 0.
  • Streak does not reset on logout.
  • Active streaks are publicly visible to all players on the server.

Kill Streak Display System

Each player's current kill streak is displayed beside their name in-person only — not on the tab list. The display updates live with the exact streak number, not just milestones. A player on 21 kills shows [21 🔥], not [20 🔥].

Display rules by streak range:

  • Under 5 kills — nothing is shown
  • 5 to 14 kills — [X 🔥] in orange
  • 15 to 24 kills — [X 🔥] in purple, symbolizing a higher tier of threat
  • 25 to 30 kills — [X 🔥] in blood red, slightly larger text, indicating elite status

Plugin Concept

The main idea of this plugin is for players that love the idea of pvp, wars and battles, so they can express their skill and strength, but.. its also made for the players that love grinding, try to ignore pvp, and just want to live in peace while feeling and BEING POWERFUL! Ascension is all about ascending from being nothing, to being everything.

Concept

Ascension is a pvp & grinding-based plugin where players have to either get a specific amount of kill streak to earn rebirth points which lets them unlock various abilities or grind them. you can also earn rebirth points by crafting them using their custom recipe which is found in our discord server, when rebirthed you will be met with a menu and 3 abilities which you can pick 1 of them. with each rebirth, the abilities get better and better. There are 18 abilities and 6 rebirth levels which we plan on upgrading in the future.

Commands & Utilities

⚔️ Combat & Mechanics

Command What it does
/streakOpens a GUI menu showing your current kill streak and how many kills you need for the next Rebirth Point.
/streak-toggleToggles whether your streak tag (e.g., [5 🔥]) is visible above your head to other players.
/rebirthOpens the Rebirth Panel to spend Rebirth Points and select new Ascension Abilities.
/ability-set <player> <ability>(Admin) Forcefully sets a player's active Ascension Ability.

🏠 Teleportation & Homes

Command What it does
/homeOpens the GUI Home Panel to set, teleport to, rename, or delete your homes.
/tpa <player>Sends a teleport request to another player.
/tpacceptAccepts a pending teleport request.
/tpdenyDenies a pending teleport request.
/rtpTeleports you to a random safe location in the world (3000 block radius).
/backReturns you to the location of your last natural death.

🛡️ Social & Teams

Command What it does
/team create <name>Start a new team.
/team invite <player>Invite someone to join your team.
/team accept <team>Join a team you were invited to.
/team chatToggle between global and private team chat.
/team leave / disband / kick / rename / promoteManage your team and its members.
/msg <player> <message>Sends a private message to another player.
/reply <message>Quick-reply to the last person who messaged you.
