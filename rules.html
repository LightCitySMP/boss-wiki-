LightCitySMP — Consitution

✦

We advise you to read all of the laws of our SMP, to make sure you never break any. consequences are severe if you ever break a law.

✦
⚔️ PvP 7 laws
› Using tridents or elytras in a 1v1 is BANNED — but allowed in 1v2s+.
› Elytra macing using fireworks and trident macing are BANNED — but elytra macing normally is allowed.
› If you get out of combat timer while fighting someone, you are NOT ALLOWED to use /home, /team home, /rtp, /tpa, /spawn, or any escape command — unless you're far enough away that your opponent has lost track of you.
› Using pearl chambers during a duel or fight is BANNED.Quick transportation outside of combat is fine.
› Naked killing is STRICTLY BANNED and may result in a permanent ban.
› TP trapping and TP killing are allowed.
› TNT, TNT minecarts, and beds are NOT ALLOWED in PvP — but can be used for trapping.
◆
🏗️ Building & Griefing 5 laws
› Destroying or blowing up bases is STRICTLY BANNED.
› Raiding bases is allowed.
› Bases built below Y -50 are ILLEGAL.
› NSFW builds or structures of any kind are STRICTLY BANNED.
◆
🤝 Alliances & Wars 3 laws
› Alliances are only permitted for trading or non-aggression pacts — teaming up against another team is BANNED. Only 1v1 team fights are allowed.
› Fight your wars in the desert to preserve the natural environment of LightCitySMP.
› Killing villagers or animals owned by other players is BANNED.Stealing them is allowed.
◆
🛡️ Cheating & Exploiting 5 laws
› ESP, Xray, seed cracking, abusing bugs, and abusing glitches are EXTREMELY BANNED and will result in a long-term ban.
› Any cheating client will result in a PERMANENT BAN.
› Banned mods: Health indicators, minimaps that reveal players or mobs, or any mod providing unfair positional advantage, Freecam and replaymod
› Chunk loaders of any kind are BANNED.
› Lag machines are BANNED. Any intentional construction designed to degrade server performance will result in immediate punishment.
◆
📜 General Conduct 2 laws
› Toxicity is allowed but has its limits. Don't go too far.
› If you don't have fun, then create it.
